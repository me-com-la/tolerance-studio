The pipeline

Brief in. Checked imagery out.

Your product photos anchor everything — they're what every render is checked against.

01
Photos & brief

1–3 real product photos plus your rules, ranked by weight: what must be true in every image, what should be, and what's just flavor.

02
Scenes

A scene list to your brand's palette, light, and mood — one locked look across the whole batch, so 300 images feel like one shoot.

03
Generation

AI draws your product into each scene, guided by your photos and your weighted rules. Range is the point: angles, settings, and interactions you never photographed.

04
Check & review

Every render is machine-checked against your real photos — shape, pattern, color, branding. Failures rerun automatically. A human signs off before you see anything.
