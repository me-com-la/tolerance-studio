Accuracy checklist · adapted

Texture & form, not engineering spec

  • Form — the actual silhouette and proportions of the real piece, not an invented vase.
  • Material / grain — real wood grain and finish; reads as the handmade object, not generic CG.
  • Color / tone — true to the piece.
  • Consistency — lighting, surface, and camera feel identical across all 5 so they read as one collection.
  • No phantom features — no carving, joint, or detail the real piece doesn't have.
Rule: consistency is the binary bar here, drift across the set is a fail even if each image is nice alone.
