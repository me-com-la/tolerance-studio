The 7-point brand inspection.

Every image we deliver is scored against your real product on seven fixed points — the same seven, every brand, every time. Not a vibe score. A receipt you can check.

1. Shape

Silhouette, structure, and proportions match your product.

2. Color

Colors match the real thing.

3. Materials

Surface, texture, sheen, and pattern read true.

4. Details

Logos, hardware, closures, seams, and text — correct and undistorted.

5 Clarity

Sharp and clean — no artifacts or warping.

6. Lighting

Believable light direction, shadows, and reflections.

7. Scene

The product sits naturally in its setting, not pasted on.

7/7 verified

"Structure, color, and hardware all check out — ships as-is."

The honest part — Exact render

What Exact render can't do

  • The scene angle is your photo's angle. A new angle needs a new photo — not more prompting.
  • Nothing can pass in front of the product unless it was photographed with it.
  • Fine detail (thin stems, wisps) needs a clean, sharp source photo — the photo is the product.
