Toyota

Thousands of parts with no studio shot and no realistic way to shoot them all. We built the imagery from engineering files instead, and held every part exact to spec. This is the work the rest of Tolerance Studio is built on.

What "correct" means here

Accuracy isn't a style. It's the product.

  • Geometry — dimensions and proportions match the real part. No warping.
  • Material — metal reads as metal, with the right grain and weight.
  • Finish — gloss, matte, anodize, or coating matches the actual SKU.
  • Hardware — correct bolts, threads, and connectors. Nothing invented or dropped.
  • Badging — part numbers and marks stay legible and correct.
Rule: one wrong bolt, thread, or number and the image is rejected. Spec is pass or fail, never close enough.
